What is a combination?

Order does NOT matter.
{A, B, C} is the same group as {C, B, A}.

When do you use combinations?

  • Choosing groups or teams
  • Selecting items
  • Order does not change the result

What do n and r mean?

n = total choices available
r = number you choose
